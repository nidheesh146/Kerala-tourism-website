Places to Visit

1.Beaches

Varkala beach

Varkala beach is the only place in southern Kerala where cliffs are found adjacent to the Arabian Sea.

Cherai beach

Cherai Beach is near to Vypeen Island in Ernakulam district of Kerala. It is an ideal spot for swimming and viewing the Chinese fishing nets or Cheena Vala.

Kappad beach

Located 16 km south of Kozhikode, Kerala, India, Kappad Beach, also known as Kappakadavu, is a serene and tranquil destination

Samudra beach

Located 16 km south of Kozhikode, Kerala, India, Kappad Beach, also known as Kappakadavu, is a serene and tranquil destination

Marari beach

Located in Mararikulam, Kerala, India, Marari Beach Resort is a 55-acre property sprawled across lawns, lotus ponds, and coconut groves.

2.Hillstations

Munnar

Munnar situated at the confluence of three mountain streams is one of the most popular hill stations located in Idukki district, Kerala.

Wayanad

Wayanad is a district in the north-east of the Indian state of Kerala, with administrative headquarters at the municipality of Kalpetta. It is the only plateau in Kerala.

Vagamon

Vagamon is a tiny plantation township in Central Travancore, Vagamon has an overtone of green hill station

Thekkady

Thekkady is situated about 257 km (160 mi) from Trivandrum, 145 km from Cochin International Airport and 114 km from Kottayam railway station.

Ponmudi

Meenmutty Falls, Thiruvananthapuram is one of the main attractions located nearby Ponmudi hills and is around 45 km from Thiruvananthapuram

3.Waterfalls

Athirapally Waterfalls

Located in Athirapilly, Chalakudy Taluk, Thrissur District, Kerala, India, Athirappilly Falls is a segmented waterfall with a total height of 25 meters (82 ft) and a longest drop of 51 meters (167 ft).

Vazhachal Waterfalls

Vazhachal Falls is situated in Athirappilly Panchayath of Thrissur district in Kerala on the southwest coast of India

Palaruvi Waterfalls

Lying 50 km from Kozhikode, the Thusharagiri Waterfalls comprises Erattumukku, Mazhavil Chattom, and Thumbithullum Para which can be reached through trekking.

Thusharagiri Waterfalls

Lying 50 km from Kozhikode, the Thusharagiri Waterfalls comprises Erattumukku, Mazhavil Chattom, and Thumbithullum Para which can be reached through trekking.

Soochipara waterfalls

Located in the Vellarimala region of Wayanad district, Kerala, India, Soochipara Waterfalls, also known as Sentinel Rock Waterfalls, is a breathtaking three-tiered waterfall surrounded by lush evergreen forests.
